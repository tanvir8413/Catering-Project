Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?
We offer full-service catering for events including weddings, corporate meetings, birthdays, and more.
Do you provide vegetarian options?
Yes! We provide a variety of vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free dishes to suit your needs.
How far in advance should I book?
Ideally, bookings should be made 2-3 weeks in advance to ensure availability.
Can I customize the menu?
Absolutely. We offer customizable menus tailored to your event and preferences.
